About Us

A nurse-led practice built around clinical oversight you can trust.

Concierge Healthcare Coordination is a nurse-led consulting and case management practice dedicated to supporting individuals, families, and professional advisors navigating today's increasingly complex healthcare landscape.

As board-certified Registered Nurse Case Managers with extensive experience in hospital case management, emergency medicine, special needs care, and interdisciplinary care coordination, we provide objective, comprehensive clinical oversight — ensuring each client's medical needs, safety considerations, and overall quality of life are thoughtfully assessed and continuously monitored.

Services

Thoughtful, coordinated care — every step of the way.

From crisis moments to long-term planning, we step in with clinical judgment and calm, personal attention.

Level-of-Care Transition Support

Education, assistance, and support for transitions across all levels of care — assisted living, long-term care, and everything in between.

Personalized Comprehensive Assessment

A thorough assessment that addresses gaps in insurance coverage, providers, benefits, home support, needed home modifications, and appropriate levels of care.

Benefits Analysis & Guidance

Post-Hospital In-Home Visit

Wellness Check-Ins

What We Offer

Your health, our priority.

A comprehensive suite of clinical services designed to support clients and their families wherever they are in the healthcare journey.

  • Comprehensive in-home or virtual assessments

    A thorough evaluation of medical needs, safety, living environment, and overall well-being, along with personalized recommendations and care planning.

  • Scheduled in-home or telephonic wellness check-ins

    Designed to monitor health status, reinforce care plans, and provide consistent support and peace-of-mind for clients and their families.

  • Responsive crisis management support

    Guidance through urgent or unexpected situations, coordinating care and helping stabilize complex circumstances when immediate assistance is needed.

  • Benefits analysis and insurance navigation

    Helping clients clearly understand coverage, identify available benefits, and access appropriate healthcare and community resources.

  • Expert guidance for level-of-care transitions

    Thoughtful planning and support for moves between hospital, rehabilitation, home care, assisted living, and long-term care settings.

  • Post-hospital discharge coordination

    Smooth transitions home, including follow-up planning, review of discharge instructions, and coordination of recommended services.

  • Home safety and medical equipment assessments

    Evaluation of living environments, recommended safety improvements, and ensuring appropriate equipment and supports are in place.

  • Ongoing communication with healthcare providers

    Keeping every member of the care team informed, aligned, and focused on achieving the best possible outcomes.
